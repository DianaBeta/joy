French Toasts recipe

This is the perfect breakfast recipe to enjoy alone or with friends.

By Diana Lucia Betancourt

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk

Method


First mix the eggs in a bowl. (The bow should be big enough for the bread slices to fit) Subsecuently add the milk into the egg mixture. Now yøu have the mixture ready. Now take one the bread slices and dip it into the mixture. Be careful to not leave it inside too long.
