French Toasts recipe

This is the perfect breakfast recipe to enjoy alone or with friends.

Diana Lucia Betancourt

Ingredients (5 Servings)


  • 2 eggs

  • 1 cup of milk

  • 5 Slices of bread

  • A teaspoon of cinammon

  • A teaspoon of vanilla extract or vanilla sugar

Method


  1. First mix the eggs, mill, cinnamon, sugar and vanilla in a bowl (The bow should be big enough for the bread slices to fit).

  2. Now grease a pan with a little bit of butter and put on the Stove on low heat.

  3. Now take one the bread slices and dip it into the mixture.Be careful to not leave it inside too long, that will make the bread soggy.

  4. Place the dipped bread in the pan for about two minutes or unti it is toasted and turn it around.
